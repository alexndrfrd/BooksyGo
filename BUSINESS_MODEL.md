|---------------|-----------------|
| 0-5 | Standard rate |
| 6-10 | +0.5% bonus |

- Premium: ‚Ç¨9.99/month (not ‚Ç¨10)

### Seasonal Pricing

- **Peak Season** (June-Aug, Dec): Normal rates

- **Off-Season** (Jan-Feb): 20% discount campaigns
